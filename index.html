HOW TO PLAY DARK BATTLEMAGE Strike builds 15 Essence and can proc Overflow. Rend (20) applies a 12s bleed — keep it rolling. Ruin (40) is your big spender. Riposte is free and huge, but only while Overflow glows — 8s to use it. Onslaught (45s CD) doubles Essence gain for 10s. Interrupt has no cooldown — but kick empty air and it locks for 6s. Cataclysm charges from damage — quality damage charges faster. TEMPEST ADEPT Jolt builds 15 Static. Conduit (10) is a bleed whose ticks can refresh Stormlance — a hard hitter with 2 charges. Overcharge (free, 20s CD) empowers your next two Jolts by 150%. Maelstrom dumps 30–60 Static — bigger dumps hit harder per point. Ultimate Tempest: 30% faster globals for 30 seconds. THE FIGHT Exposed: boss takes +50% — burn everything. Carapace: direct hits bounce for zero — but Rend slips through the shell. Pool Essence, keep the bleed rolling, touch nothing else. Casts: interrupt or be Sundered. Miss two and you die. Enrage at 4:00 kills you. SCORING Your parse is graded after the fight. The autopsy tells you exactly what to fix.
